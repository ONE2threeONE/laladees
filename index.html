Born from a Sicilian grandmother who cooked like the world depended on it and a grandfather from the north of Italy who showed them that it does. At LaLa Dee's, every evening is a celebration of handmade pastas, the finest aged cuts, and ocean-fresh seafood, prepared with the love of two cousins and the traditions of a family that has always known how to feed people.
